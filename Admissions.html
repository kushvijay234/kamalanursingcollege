ADMISSION CRITERIA

  • The candidate must have passed +2/ HSC with science including Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English
  • A candidate must have minimum 17 years of age.
  • Must be domicile of Maharashtra and passed SSC & HSC in Maharashtra state only
  • Minimum marks/ percentage is 40% for reserved category and 45% for open/ EWS category
  • And as per the rules of INC, MUHS, DMER Broucher, CET Cell and ARA.
