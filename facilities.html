  • Library

    In current trend, knowledge is the soul of education and books are the quantum of knowledge. Hence our institute believes that library is the core of all information.

    Our library is equipped with more than 3000 books to quench the thirst of knowledge, which students can utilize to improve the magnitude of information in all aspect. It also has numerous journal, literature, health magazines etc.

    Library is equipped with adequate space, well lighted and ventilated room, comfortable furniture.

  • Transport

    Our institute has got its own vehicle for transportation which is used for various curricular activities such as rural public health posting, Urban Public Health Posting, Hospital Posting. It is also used to conduct educational visits as per curriculum such as:

    • Water Purification Plant
    • Milk Dairy
    • Orphanages
    • Old Age House etc.
  • Hostel

    Our institute provides Hostel facility for student with

    • Clean accommodation
    • Hot & Cold water supply
    • Hygiene food
    • 24 x 7 Uninterrupted Power Supply

    Along with this our hostel has got recreation room, dining hall, visitor's room, room for the sick & adequate security. Independent play ground where sports like ring, throw ball, badminton are played which gives students ample of physical exercise which supports a sound body.

  • Function Hall

    Separate function halls are available. Each accommodates more than 200 members at a time where various cultural & educational activities are conducted.
