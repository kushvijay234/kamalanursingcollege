  • Nutrition Lab

    One of the important aspects of patient care is nutrition. The lab enables the students to gain knowledge & importance of Nutrition in health. Lab is well equipped with all facilities so that student can prepare therapeutic diets.

  • Nursing Lab

    This is the essentials of nursing. Students are oriented to various biological & behavioral sciences. Correct Procedures regarding patient care & proper methods to attend patients with different ailments, are demonstrated by skilled staffs.

  • Community Health Lab

    B. Sc Nursing course is mainly focused to improve the health care delivery system in rural & urban areas. This part of education takes the responsibility of orienting students to the needs of community at different levels of prevention. A blend of theory & on field experience enables students to contribute to the poorer section of society by employing community health care principles. Students also participate in implementing national rural health programs through RPH & UPH posting.

  • Maternal Child Health Lab

    This lab enables the understanding of birth process, pregnancy, labor & post natal phase. A thorough understanding of maternity cycle, anatomy & physiological changes during pregnancy enables the students to provide complete care for woman. Students are also taught to identify high risk cases & skills to manage such cases.
